How it works

Not an app. Not a spreadsheet. A trainer you talk to in whatever chat you already use.

1

Meet your trainer

Not at the gym — just a casual conversation about you, your goals, and how hard you want to be pushed. The agent builds your program from that.

2

Lift

Log your sets, ask what's next, or just lift and review later. The agent adapts to how you want to use it — silent logging, step-by-step guidance, or anything between.

3

Get better

The agent tracks your progress, spots plateaus, adjusts your program, and coaches you between sets when you need it. You don't think about the plan — it does.

What makes it different

It's not a workout tracker. It's a trainer that knows you.

It's an expert

Knows form, technique, exercise selection, and how to program around lifts. It infers exercises from context and confirms when unsure. It doesn't need to be told what a bench press is.

Adapts to you

How hard to push, how to handle a bad day, whether to proactively tell you the next set or stay quiet — all based on who you are, not a template. Some people want to be pushed, some want gentle. It learns which.

Lives in your chat

Not a new app to download. It lives in whatever chat app you already use — Telegram, Discord, wherever your agent lives. You already have it open at the gym.
